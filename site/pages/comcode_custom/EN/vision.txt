    			<li>
    				<span>7</span>

    			<li>

    	<h2 class="sub-head-big">Standards &amp; accessibility</h2>

    	<p>One key factor we emphasise is the growing importance of accessibility on the Internet. For the web to progress, we all (website developers) share the obligation to build semantically-correct websites. This essential consideration ensures that innovative new software can interact with websites in clever ways, and that everyone-including paralysed and blind users, can fully participate on the web.</p>

    	<p>Sadly, widespread support is largely lacking within the CMS industry. Composr, contrast currently conforms to <a href="{$PAGE_LINK*,docs:tut_markup}">dozens of strict standards</a> &mdash; something for which we are very proud.</p>
